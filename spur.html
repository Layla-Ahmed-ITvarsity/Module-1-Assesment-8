Spur Steak Ranches

Spur is a family-friendly restaurant known for its mouth-watering steaks, burgers, ribs, and a fun dining experience. With a strong South African heritage, Spur offers something for everyone—from sizzling grills to fresh salads, tasty desserts, and a dedicated kids’ menu. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or just craving a hearty meal, Spur is all about good food, great company, and warm hospitality.
