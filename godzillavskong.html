GODZILLA VS KONG
PG 13 1h 53m Violence

In a world where colossal monsters roam the Earth, two legendary titans face off in an epic battle that will shake the planet to its core. Godzilla, the king of the monsters, emerges from the depths to challenge Kong, the mighty protector of Skull Island. As humanity watches in awe and fear, the two powerhouses engage in a fierce clash of strength, skill, and survival. But as the battle rages on, hidden forces emerge, threatening to destroy both beasts and mankind. It's a spectacular showdown where only one can reign supreme. Will Godzilla's raw power triumph, or will Kong's intelligence and resilience make him the true king of monsters?
